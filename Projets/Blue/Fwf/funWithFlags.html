Fun with flags

This project was carried out during the first semester of the preparatory year at the ECAL with Romain Collaud. We had to get used to using Figma and designing interactive flags.

So I looked at the very essence of a flag: representing a country. Some, like the flag of Switzerland or France, represent the values of the country. Others, like the flag of Cyprus, directly represent the country itself.

That's what inspired me. I also took the landscape view of the flag of Kiribati (an archipelago in the Pacific Ocean) and mixed it with the top view of the Cypriot flag.

I wanted to sequence and evolve these flags, so that with each interaction you discover a little more about the country or region. Whether it's the grid system that makes us think of the road, or the noise indicated by the typography or the buildings.
