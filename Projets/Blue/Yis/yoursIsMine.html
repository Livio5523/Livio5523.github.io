Yours is mine

This project was carried out during the preparatory year at ECAL. The theme "Yours is mine was given during the "image workshop" course and and we were free to choose the technique.

I decided to represent my friends in a 3D construction. Each part represents a feeling I have for them or an experience experience we've had together.

I think that everyone around us shapes us and, in a way, every moment spent with them is precious for both of us. In a friendship, everything is shared.
