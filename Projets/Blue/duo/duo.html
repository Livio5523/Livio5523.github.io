DUO

"Duo" is a storytelling project that I'd had in mind for some time. It fitted in perfectly with the 'duo' theme I'd been working on in the 'Atelier de l'image' course at the ECAL.

This project allowed me to bring to life bring to life characters that I had mentally created a long time ago.


"Alex is an ordinary boy who dreams of becoming a dancer, even though he's not particularly talented. One day, on his way home from he steps in a puddle. Before he knows it, it's a little slimy creature, a kind of puddle. slimy creature, a sort of puddle. The puddle grows little by little, taking up more and more space, until it engulfs Alex completely. This encounter will change his life forever. The creature known as "Viscose" quickly becomes his his best friend, even though he can't tame her yet. Together they have many adventures that will lead him to become an outstanding dancer".
