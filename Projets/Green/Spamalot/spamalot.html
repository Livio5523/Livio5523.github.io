SPAMALOT

In the summer of 2023, my friend Tania Vega contacted me about a project. I had to create images that would be projected during a musical and used as a backdrop.


The project was part of a graduation show at the Evaprod school of musical theatre, based in La Chaux-de-Fonds. So I threw myself into the project and created five 3D sets using Blender.


It was a real challenge to create an environment that had the atmosphere of a musical. It was also the first time I'd directed scenes in 3D for someone else. We were able to compare our ideas and I had to adapt my vision to better suit the musical.

We're having fun at La Tchaux!
